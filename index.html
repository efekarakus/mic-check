Communication

In League of Legends, players need to share information with each other in order to kill opponents, or take objectives. Effective communication leads to players knowing which steps to take next, and thus wins.

We recorded the voices of 3 of our players in a game of LoL, where each bar represents a second of the game. Our goal is to explore different aspects of interactions and show the potential of speech analysis in competitive video games.
